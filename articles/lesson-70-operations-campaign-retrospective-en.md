# Lesson 70: Operations Assistant for Campaigns and Retrospectives

Operations work combines ideas, coordination, and data review. OpenClaw can turn campaigns into structured workflows instead of scattered tasks.

## Skills Used

Search [skills.lc](https://skills.lc/) for `marketing`, `campaign`, `analytics`, or `content`. In this environment, use `documents` for campaign plans, `spreadsheets` for schedules and metrics, `presentations` for retrospectives, and `imagegen` for asset directions.

## Workflow

Before the campaign, write a brief: goal, audience, message, channel, timeline, assets, budget, risks, and metrics.

During the campaign, maintain a content calendar with status, owner, publish time, and acceptance criteria.

After the campaign, review conversion, cost, retention, feedback, and anomalies. Do not stop at impressions.

## Human Review

Brand language, discounts, partner rights, user data, and public commitments need human approval.

## Exercise

Ask OpenClaw to create a launch plan, seven-day content calendar, and retrospective template for a new feature.

The assistant keeps actions aligned with goals.
