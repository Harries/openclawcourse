# Lesson 65: Podcast and Interview Workflows with OpenClaw

The core of a podcast is conversation quality. OpenClaw can help before recording with questions, and after recording with transcripts, summaries, and publishing assets.

## Skills Used

Search [skills.lc](https://skills.lc/) for `podcast`, `interview`, `transcription`, or `summary`. In this environment, use `openai-whisper` or `openai-whisper-api` for audio transcription, `summarize` for summaries, `documents` for interview guides, and `sag` for voice tests.

## Workflow

Before recording, generate an interview map: main questions, follow-ups, sensitive topics, and fact-check points. Leave room for stories.

After recording, transcribe the full audio and clean verbal noise. Do not treat a transcript as a finished article.

Generate content assets: show notes, timeline, quotes, social posts, newsletter version, and title options.

## Human Review

Company names, product data, confidential information, and quotable statements should be confirmed before publishing.

## Exercise

Prepare an interview with an OpenClaw Skill author. Ask OpenClaw for 12 questions, a post-recording summary template, and publishing copy.

The assistant turns conversations into reusable content.
