# Lesson 68: Building a Customer Support Assistant with OpenClaw

The goal is not to automate as many replies as possible. The goal is to answer faster, answer correctly, and know when to escalate.

## Skills Used

Search [skills.lc](https://skills.lc/) for `support`, `knowledge base`, `helpdesk`, or `rag`. In this environment, use `documents` for knowledge-base content, `browser` to verify help docs, `slack` or `discord` for internal coordination, and `notion` or `obsidian` for knowledge entries.

## Workflow

Structure the knowledge base: product issues, account issues, billing, troubleshooting, refund policies, and escalation paths. Each entry needs scope and update time.

For each customer question, classify it, retrieve relevant knowledge, and draft a reply with a concise answer, steps, and missing information.

Escalate refunds, complaints, privacy, security, legal issues, and uncertain answers.

## Exercise

Give OpenClaw ten user questions and an FAQ. Ask for reply drafts, classification tags, and an escalation list.

A reliable support assistant knows when it should not answer.
