# Lesson 69: Sales Assistant, CRM Notes, and Follow-Up Emails

A sales assistant should not sell for you. It should preserve context, prepare conversations, and prevent follow-up loss.

## Skills Used

Search [skills.lc](https://skills.lc/) for `sales`, `crm`, `email`, or `lead`. In this environment, use `himalaya` or `gog` for email, `spreadsheets` for lead lists, `notion` or `trello` for opportunities, and `browser` for account research.

## Workflow

Create an account profile: company, role, pain, budget, decision chain, last conversation, commitments, and risks.

Before a call, generate preparation notes: questions, demo focus, and likely objections. After the call, generate follow-up email and CRM update. Separate facts, assumptions, and next actions.

Set follow-up reminders for important opportunities.

## Human Review

Pricing, discounts, contract terms, and delivery commitments require human approval.

## Exercise

Give OpenClaw a customer conversation record and ask for a customer profile, follow-up email, and CRM update.

The assistant helps every conversation move forward with context.
